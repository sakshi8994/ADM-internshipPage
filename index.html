Internships

Our internship opportunities cover a broad spectrum of fields to cater to the diverse talents and interests of our participants. These internship roles are designed to empower students, housewives, and individuals from all backgrounds to develop valuable skills and gain practical experience. We believe in providing opportunities for personal growth and meaningful contributions to society. The certificates we distribute upon completion of these internships represent not only a milestone in your personal and professional journey but also your active role in shaping a brighter future for yourself and the community. Join us in this incredible journey of knowledge, growth, and empowerment, whether through our certificate programs or by participating in our dynamic internships. Your achievements and contributions are our inspiration, and together, we can work towards shaping a more educated, empowered, and capable world.

Domains of Internship

Human Resources

The duties of an HR intern include updating employee databases, gathering payroll data, screening resumes, scheduling interviews, posting job ads, and assisting with HR-related reports and employee queries.

Project Manager

Project Managers play a crucial role in guiding and motivating project teams to achieve project objectives. They are responsible for identifying project scope, budgets, schedules, and risks. Additionally, they develop and execute project plans, monitoring progress and making necessary adjustments

CSR Intern

CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) personnel identify, measure, and monitor the social impact of an organization's activities. They influence the organization's culture by identifying CSR initiatives and engage with external bodies like charities and community groups.

Marketing

A marketing intern assists a company in its marketing and advertising efforts. The marketing intern may be involved in many tasks, including identifying marketing needs, designing marketing materials, and working with team members to evaluate current marketing efforts.

Browse
Public Relation

Professionals in Public Relations write and produce presentations, articles, press releases, and media posts. They handle public and press inquiries, speak at press conferences, and analyze media coverage

Fundraising

As a Fundraising Intern, you'll help organize and execute fundraising campaigns and events to support our community initiatives. Collaborate with our team to engage donors, create promotional materials, and gain hands-on experience in nonprofit fundraising.

UX Design

UI/UX designers are generalists who participate in all phases of the design process. These professionals are skilled in user research, ideation, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

Graphic Design

The objective of the Graphic Design Intern is to design and produce content for the website, social media, public display, and other mediums. Create and maintain graphic design content for branded communications and marketing tools. Assist in photo editing for website & other print materials.

Full Stack Developer

As a web development intern, you will actively participate in designing, developing, and maintaining websites and web applications. This involves writing clean and efficient code using languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.

Video Editors creatively edit video content for publication on social platforms. They assemble raw footage, select shots based on quality and content relevance, and add music, dialogues, graphics, and effects for a polished final product.
